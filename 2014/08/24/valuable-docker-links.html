Valuable Docker Links

Latest update: 2014-08-24

Tags

Share

The Docker weekly email newsletter is a great service to stay tuned with Docker on a weekly basis. You will find great links, news, articles, howtos, manuals and so concerning Docker and its evolving ecosystem. Nevertheless, these informations are short-living. They are fading out of focus as the weeks pass by. But there are a lot of great posts which should be collected and presented in a more enduring way.

This page will capture some important links I discovered following the weekly Docker newsletter. I use these links the most in my day by day business as a cloud computing researcher trying to overcome cloud service provider generated vendor lock-ins.

The links on this site are evaluated weekly (very subjective). So links may be added if I find new interesting online ressources. Links may be replaced if I find better online ressources. And links may be deleted if I think they are outdated or no longer useful at all.

Feel free to contact me if you think there are valuable links missing or if you know better ones. You may also want to use the disqus comments an the end of this article to propose improvements or additional or even better links.

Warning: Links are not selected by objective criteria (this collection only contains links I find useful from a pure personal opinion).
