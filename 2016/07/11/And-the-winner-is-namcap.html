And the winner is ... NAMCAP

11 Jul 2016

We had some fun this summer semester webtechnology course. My students implemented several classical games as Dart single page HTML ‘apps’.

Three games (of 13 game submissions) entered the Webtech Hall of Fame.

  • #1: Namcap by Sheraz Azad, Malte Grebe, Niklas Klatt
  • #2: Kisten Schieben by Tim Cedric Korinth, Johann Michaelis, Felix Richter, Malte Schreiber
  • #3: Tank Game by Sönke Gonschorowski, Jannik Kühnemundt

Congratulations.
