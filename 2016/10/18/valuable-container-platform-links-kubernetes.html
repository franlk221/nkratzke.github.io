Valuable Kubernetes Links

14 Mar 2018 , first release: 18 Oct 2016 Frans Berkelaar / flickr.com, Lizenz: CC BY 2.0

Elastic container platforms like Kubernetes get more and more attention due to the fact that they enable and simplify the deployment and operation of multi-container systems or cloud-native applications.

All these platforms provide weekly email newsletters where great links, news, articles, howtos, manuals and more can be found. Nevertheless, these informations are short-living. They are fading out of focus as the weeks pass by. But there are a lot of great posts which should be collected and presented in a more enduring way.

The links on this site are reviewed and updated weekly (very subjective).

Feel free to contact me if you think there are valuable links missing or if you know better links. You may also want to use the disqus comments at the end of this article to propose improvements or additional or even better links.

Warning: Links are not selected by objective criteria (this collection only contains links I find useful from a very personal point of view).
