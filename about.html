That's me Nane Kratzke

I am a computer scientist, cloud nerd, open sourcer, and professor at the Lübeck University of Applied Sciences, Germany.

This is my personal site to share (open source) content from my teaching or researching activities I assume to be of general interest for colleagues in higher education, researchers, (former) students of mine, or (cloud) software engineers.

Research Interests

Im an interested in

  • model-based software and system design
  • model-based analysis of (distributed) systems
  • cloud computing (in general but especially infrastructure as a service)
  • model based cloud service design approaches (using lightweight virtualization/containerization)

Teaching Activities

Further information on courses I give can be found at the corresponding study programs. In short my teaching activities include:

  • programming for freshman
  • cloud computing
  • databases
  • webtechnologies (client/server side programming)
  • enterprise architectures
