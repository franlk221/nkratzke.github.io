That's me Nane Kratzke

Computer scientist, cloud nerd, open sourcer, and professor at Lübeck University of Applied Sciences, Germany.

This is my personal site to share (open source) content from my teaching or researching activities I assume to be of general interest for colleagues in higher education, researchers, (former) students of mine, or (cloud) software engineers.

Research Interests

I am interested in

  • model-based software and system design
  • web information systems
  • cloud computing
  • model based cloud service design approaches

Teaching Activities

Further information on lectures I give can be found at the corresponding study programs. In short my teaching activities include:

  • programming for freshman
  • cloud computing
  • databases
  • webtechnologies
  • enterprise architectures
